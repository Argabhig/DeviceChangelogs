|===| Rom Flashing Steps For kernel 4.14 |===|
• Must be on A10 FW ( recommended global stable)
• Reboot to recovery.
• Format Data , Wipe System, Vendor, Data ,Cache ( in short clean flash)
• Flash 4.14 Rom
• Flash GAPPs ( Nik or Bit ) (Optional)
• Flash ofox 4.14 recovery
• Reboot.
• Flash Magisk cu only after first boot.

• To go back 4.4 rom:
  - clean flash 4.4 rom and flash official ofox which is based on 4.4 kernel
